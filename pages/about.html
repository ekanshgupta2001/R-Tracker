About This Tool

R-Tracker is a high-performance pathing visualizer designed to bridge the gap between code and the field. It allows teams to simulate autonomous paths and refine driver practice in a digital environment, saving hours of on-field calibration. This project was spearheaded by Ekansh Gupta and co-designed by Neel Laroiya of Team 25702 Rundle Robotics Castle.

Project Legacy

We believe that the best way to learn is to build. R-Tracker represents our commitment to high-level software engineering in FTC. By maintaining this tool, we provide a consistent standard for driver training and autonomous logic that future team members can build upon, fork, and improve.

Future Improvements

R-Tracker is constantly evolving. Our development roadmap includes:

Multi-Pathing Functionality:

Currently focused on Pedro Pathing, we are working to expand support for various autonomous control frameworks to make R-Tracker more versatile.

Cloud Syncing:

Real-time collaboration tools to allow multiple teams or members to design and sync paths simultaneously across different devices.
