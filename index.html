About R-Tracker

R-Tracker is a browser-based driver practice and path planning tool built for FTC teams competing in the 2025–2026 DECODE season. Practice with a real gamepad or keyboard, tune your robot config, and prepare for competition — no robot required.

Built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No installation needed — just open and drive.
